## üéØ ENVIRONMENTAL METRICS

### Calculated for Each Material
- **AI Score**: 0-100% composite score
- **Eco-Score**: Environmental rating based on:
  - Biodegradability (30%)
  - Recyclability (30%)
  - CO2 Emissions (20%)
  - Cost (10%)
  - Strength (10%)

- **Carbon Footprint**: kg CO2 per unit weight
- **CO2 Reduction %**: Compared to baseline plastic
- **Cost Savings %**: Economic benefit
- **Sustainability Rating**: 0-100 comprehensive metric
- **Recyclability %**: Post-consumer recyclability
- **Biodegradability Score**: 1-10 rating
- **Strength Rating**: 1-10 rating
- **Cost per kg**: USD/kg
